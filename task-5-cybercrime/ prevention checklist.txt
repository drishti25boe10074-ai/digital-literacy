prevention checklist 

1.	Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be bank officials. (UPI safety) 
2.	Always check whether you are sending money or receiving money before entering your UPI PIN in any payment app. (UPI safety) 
3.	Do not click suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, email, or social media. 
4.	Use strong and unique passwords for your email, banking apps, and social media accounts. 
5.	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible for extra account security. 
6.	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or important personal accounts. 
7.	Download apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or official websites. 
8.	Keep your phone and laptop updated with antivirus software and system updates. 
9.	Do not share personal information like Aadhaar number, bank details, or college ID publicly online. 
10.	Always verify unknown calls claiming to be from banks, delivery agents, or technical support before responding.
