## Do’s ✅
- Use respectful and professional language in posts and comments.
- Verify information before sharing it online.
- Protect your personal information and privacy settings.
- Share academic achievements and positive activities.
- Think carefully before posting content that represents you publicly.

## Don’ts ❌
- Do not share confidential academic or personal information.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not spread rumors or unverified news.
- Avoid oversharing your location or daily routine.
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
